Because your health journey should be just that – yours.

We're building an app to empower individual Canadians to take back control of their own health journey.

With nearly 1 in every 4 Canadians lacking a primary care physician, and a fragmented health and wellness experience overall, it's time for a better way.

Our platform will allow for all types of health and wellness data to be saved in one fortified place and – upon voluntary opt-in – to be analyzed by a secure AI to surface valuable insights.

We're building Stowe Health for all Canadians, including...

Individuals

Who want to actually understand their health and manage it more easily

Caregivers

Who are tasked with maintaining both their own health and others' health too

Parents

Who need to maintain a complete, portable record for their kids

Proactive planners

Who seek to optimize their health before something goes wrong
