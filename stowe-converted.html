Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join Stowe?

Sign up for our waitlist to get early access. Early members will be the first to experience our platform with exclusive founding member benefits and launch pricing.

Who is Stowe for?

Stowe is built for anyone who wants to take control of their health — individuals, caregivers, parents, and proactive planners. Whether you're managing your own health or caring for others, Stowe gives you the tools you need.

Where is Stowe available?

We're launching first in Canada, with plans to expand globally. Our platform works with health data from various sources and regions.

How does Stowe protect my information?

We adhere to industry-leading privacy standards with end-to-end encryption. Your data is stored securely and always controlled by you.

Does Stowe replace my doctor?

No. Stowe enhances your relationship with qualified health professionals. It doesn't diagnose or replace clinical oversight — it empowers you to detect patterns earlier and make informed decisions.
